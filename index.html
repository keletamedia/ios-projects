Mikal

Mikal is a fun iOS app I built to shuffle specific genres. The app allows you to choose upto 2 genres currently, will soon support multiple genres. You can start the radio by pressing play, along with unlimited skips of your own itunes library.

Dose

Dose is an another fun iOS app I built to get positive, daily doses. This idea came when I wanted to give myself positive reminders. It does a good job of replacing social media.
